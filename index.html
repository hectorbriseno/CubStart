My Favorite Albums

My name is Hector Briseno, these our my favorite albums of the new dedcade (2020 and onward).

Top pick for 2023 (So far):

Let's Start Here by: Lil Yachty

Let's Start Here by Lil Yachty gives listeners a drastic change of pace with his take on a psychedelic rock album with amazing production.

Top pick for 2022:

Mr. Morale & the Big Steppers by: Kendrick Lamar

Mr. Morale & the Big Steppers by Kendrick Lamar provides the story telling and lyrical content of one this best rappers of our generation with the 2-sided project showing us a even more vulnerable side of Lamar

Top pick for 2021:

Call Me If You Get Lost: Tyler, the Creator

Call Me If You Get Lost by Tyler the Creator plays homage to the mixtap area of rap, Tyler on the album is on a "victory lap" and reflects on his success but at the same time reminisces on failed attempt of love.

Top pick for 2020:

Fetch the Bolt Cutters: By Fiona Apple

Fetch the Bolt Cutters by Fiona Apple is rooted in experimentation, giving listeners a interesting and engaging sonic experience all the way through with profound songwriting.
